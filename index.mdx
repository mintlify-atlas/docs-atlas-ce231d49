Kiosplay is a web-based digital signage platform that makes it easy to control what plays on your display screens. Create kiosks, upload images and videos, arrange playlists, and deploy a fullscreen looping player — all without any external database or complex infrastructure.

## How it works

Kiosplay has three main parts: the **admin panel** where you configure kiosks and playlists, the **kiosk player** that displays content fullscreen in a browser, and the **REST API** that powers both.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a kiosk">
    In the admin panel at `/admin`, create a new kiosk with a name and URL slug (e.g., `reception`, `lobby`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload media">
    Upload images and videos to the kiosk's playlist. Supported formats include JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP, MP4, WebM, and more.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Arrange the playlist">
    Drag items to reorder them, toggle visibility on individual items, and set how long each image displays.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch the player">
    Open `/:slug` (e.g., `/reception`) in a browser or point Firefox kiosk mode at that URL for a dedicated screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>
